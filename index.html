Click anywhere on the canvas to play. The water surface shows the audio waveform.
Click on a fish, it will turn orange when selected. On the next loop, a white waveform will appear for the sound from the mic, the fish will eat the sound. From the next loop the fish will play back the sound.
Click the area above the water (black area) to stop after loop ends.
The audio controls under the canvas are for debugging, don't touch.
Works best on Chrome for PC or Android. iOS as of 11.4 hiccups with recording.
